Lighting Control

The integration of lighting with other electronics is vital if you want the maximum benefit from the technology in your home.

Never come home to a dark house again. ETC can design systems that provide the right amount of light for any situation with the touch of a button. For example, one push of the away button as you leave your home can turn off all lights and the house music system, and at night pre-determined lights will turn on and off randomly to make it appear like you are home. A single press of the night button by the bed will turn off all pre-selected lights in the home. There are many lighting scenarios that can be programmed for your home that will make life easier and more enjoyable.

For security, there is a button on the keypad by the bed that turns on all exterior and interior lights. If the security system is triggered by a break-in or fire, exterior lights will flash providing visual reference for neighbors and emergency services. Interior hallway lights will turn on, illuminating the best path for your family to take for a quick escape.

These lighting systems allow your home to be more energy efficient by pre-programming your lighting needs, preventing you from leaving lights on by mistake. You will be saving time and money every day. Our engineers will work with you to review all the options to design the best lighting control system for your home, one that will maximize efficiency and enhance your lifestyle.
