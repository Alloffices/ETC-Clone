Awards

  • 1992: Bill Maronet becomes a founding Board Member of the National Home Automation Association
  • 1994: ETC is awarded the “Home of the Year” from the Home Automations Association and Electronic House magazine.
  • 1998: ETC is award the “Home of the Year” from HANA (Home Automation & Networking Association and Electronic House magazine.
  • 2001: ETC is awarded “Estate Home System of the Year” by HANA (Home Automation & Networking Association) and Electronic House magazine
  • 2004: ETC is awarded the “Luxury Home of the Year” from the National CEA (Consumer Electronics Association) and EH Publishing’s CEPro magazine
  • 2005: Bill Maronet becomes a Board Member of CEA (Consumer Electronics Association) TechHome Division.
  • 2006: ETC awarded “Integrator of the Year” by CEA (Consumer Electronics Association) and EH Publishing’s CEPro magazine.
  • 2007: Bill Maronet awarded “Industry Leader of the Year” by CEA (Consumer Electronics Association) and EH Publishing’s CEPro magazine.
  • 2009: ETC awarded a Prism Award  for “Media/Home Theater Room” by Gold Coast Builders
  • 2009: ETC awarded a Prism Award for “Automated Home of the Year” by Gold Coast Builders.
  • 2009: ETC’s Amanda Wollenweber awarded “Rookie of the Year” by Gold Coast Builders.
