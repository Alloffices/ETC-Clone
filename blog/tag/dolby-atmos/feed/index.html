]]> Dedicated home theater space is no longer a requirement for a home theater. In fact, the new outdoor theater screens are almost completely customizable and feature all the new technology available inside your home but now can be placed almost anywhere outside.

We especially like the Stealth Home Theater systems. It features LED screens, outdoor loud speakers (with new processing systems like Dolby Atmos) and the option to customize screen specifications for placement in brighter lighting including the ability to adjust the resolution and brightness. And the screens can be placed almost anywhere because of the enclosure system that will protect the screen when not in use. And an added bonus, these systems play Blu-ray movies, digital files, files stored on a network or in a cloud, cable or satellite, as well as streaming systems like Roku, Amazon Fire, Netflix, and Apple TV.

The customization of the screen and versatility of streaming sources make this theater system an easy solution for families looking for more entertainment options outside.  Interested in planning an outdoor theater for your home?  We are happy to help!

]]> A few members of our staff attended CEDIA 2014 in Denver, CO this month. It was an outstanding show.  They talked to some new vendors, met with some of our go-to vendors and saw some awesome product demos. Here are six new products and technologies presented at CEDIA 2014 to get excited about.

1. New 4K TV Choices. LG is now offering 98- and 105-inch 4K UHD LED LCD TVs through special distribution arrangements with custom installers. Read more about the new UHD TVs available November 2014.

2. Dolby Atmos. New surround sound demos were hosted by several companies and they were very impressive.  Adding Dolby Atmos to personal home theaters will provide the type of surround sound that was only available in commercial theaters.  Read more about Dolby Atmos technology for your home theater here.

3. 4K Ultra High Definition Content Options Increase. Netflix already provides streaming of native UHD programming.  During the CEDIA conference, Sony president Mike Fasulo revealed that Amazon Prime Instant Video will soon be offering 4K content. Sony has also made its 4K media player compatible with other brands of 4K UHD sets for direct 4K movie downloads, providing new content options.

4. Kaleidescape Increases Streaming Movie Options.  Kaleidescape entered into a license agreement with Sony Pictures Home Entertainment making their entire catalog of movies available for download in true Blu-ray quality.  Another big plus is the HD downloads are identical to Blu-ray including audio tracks which means Kaleidescape maybe the first (for now) only cloud-based format to deliver uncompressed Atmos soundtracks to your home theater.  Read more.

5. Leon Speakers. The new Leon custom speaker bar is outstanding, slimmer, cleaner looking, more powerful and versatile.  Leon also broadened their product line and unveiled new off-the-shelf speakers that are available at a very competitive price. Read more about Leon Speakers.

Sony’s 4K Ultra Short Throw Projector

6. Sony 4K UHD. The new line of Sony electronics touches on every aspect of 4K UHD – TVs, projectors and receivers.  The quality of the products they are producing for the luxury market are impressive.  Sony is a powerhouse brand for the discerning clientele. Read more about all the new Sony products revealed at CEDIA 2014.
