#1: Appliance Safety

We all know that leaving on lights or other appliances can waste energy. However, it can also be a safety hazard. With an automation system, you can set a specific time to turn your lights or other appliances off. Additionally, you can use your smartphone to turn off that hair straightener your daughter left on even when you’re away from home. This gives you the power to eliminate appliance hazards, and save energy by controlling lighting in your home.

#2: Increases Home Security

Imagine you’re running out the door trying to catch a flight to go on vacation. Did you remember to lock your door? With home automation, you can simply tap your finger and secure your home. No need to worry while you’re at work or on vacation. In addition, you can receive alerts if your home is entered. This ensures that your children are home from school safely, and you are aware of who enters your home at all times.

#3: Save Time and Money with a Home Automation System in West Palm Beach FL

One of the best benefits of home automation is saving time and money. Every month, you will see how having the power to control the energy being used in your home can save money on your utility bill. Additionally, the power to control security and appliances can save you time. ]]> Besides the fact that home automation is just plain cool, it will ultimately make life a bit easier and your home will be more energy efficient too!  Here’s a simple list of some of the things home automation systems can do.

  • Climate control, air conditioning and heating
  • Control lighting in the entire house
  • Control your home theater system
  • Access video cameras inside and outside your home
  • Interface with your home security system
  • Open gates, doors, windows, blinds, curtains, and any other motorized entry and exit like you garage door
  • Turn on and off any household appliance
  • Pipe in music throughout the house or to specified rooms, or the tv, or your voice

And you can do all this with the touch of a button:

  • From your phone
  • From a handheld remote while sitting in your favorite chair
  • From your computer while you are traveling
  • From your iPad
  • Automatically, based on a schedule
  • Automatically based on other “events” that happen in the home like doors opening, sun shining or motion sensors being set off

Basically, this sets up your home for things to happen without you having to get up or go from room to room to do something like turn out lights.  You can press one button that says “Night” and all the lights in the entire house are shut off.  Or press “Home”, and all the outdoor lights that lead you into the front door are lit, as well as key indoor lights.  You can monitor your energy consumption remotely and change air conditioning schedules or view all areas you monitor with security cameras.
