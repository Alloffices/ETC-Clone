CES 2015 Technology of Tomorrow

February 4th, 2015

The International Consumer Electronics Show (CES) is held in the beginning of January each year and features all types of new technology about to be released in the marketplace including TVs, phones, laptops, home appliances, cars and so much more.  We went through all the major technology blogs and compiled a list of the Best of CES 2015 just for you.

4K TV: This year these two TVs stood out amongst the crowd and garnered a lot of buzz:  LG 4K Flexible OLED and the Sharp Aquos Beyond 4K Ultra HD.   The Sharp has twice as many sub-pixels as any of the competition.  It is said it will take TV beyond 4K and is expected to be on the market by the end of 2015. Want more about all the latest in TVs? Here is a great overview.

Home Theater:  Sony continues to impress with their Short-Throw 4K projectors. This time two is better than one in this review.  Looking to cut the cable cord? This may be the way to go: Dish Sling TV

Accessory: There is plenty of buzz about this new way to power up…  Skylink Wireless PowerHub

Smartphone: This new flexible smartphone shines at CES 2015. LG G Flex2

Innovative Home Appliance: Whirlpool Smart Load Washer and Dryer that you can control from your phone.

Outdoor Audio: Amazing sound and good aesthetics makes these speakers standout.

Simple TV Fix

January 29th, 2015

One of the most common calls our service department receives is a call about TV outage.  Either the TV has sound with no picture or a picture with no sound.  We wanted to give you a helpful TV service tip in case you have any unexpected disturbances, when you least expect it!

Quite often these TV issues can be resolved by resetting the satellite receiver or cable box.  This is accomplished by powering down the respective unit and powering it back up again.  This is a quick fix. If this doesn’t work, we are always happy to send a service person out to resolve your problem quickly.
