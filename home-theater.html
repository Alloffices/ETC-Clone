Home Theater

Add the WOW factor to your home entertainment experience by taking advantage of the latest technology in home theater systems. The quality of your screen and the layout of the sound system will greatly affect the quality of your experience. There are new innovations that ETC engineers can use to make your home theater experience exceed your expectations. Our design and engineering teams consider many acoustic and aesthetic issues to achieve superior system performance and enhance the overall viewing experience: room construction parameters and dimensions, acoustical wall treatments, seating type and configuration, row height and spacing, ambient noise and light control, bass balancing, equipment and speaker placement, and more.

Additionally there are automation options that will make life simple and allow for more time to enjoy your family. For example at the touch of a button on the hand held remote, lighting and sound can be pre-set for movie screening. A DVD and CD management system can be installed to give you instant access to your collection of movies and music by artist, title or genre. Want to hide your flat screen TV when not in use? Your HDTV can be hidden in furniture and rise up on demand or it can be hidden behind a framed wall painting.

The options are numerous. ETC will help you navigate the possibilities and create the best system for you and your family.
